Instructions

Bar Controls:

  • Use the either the Left Arrow or "A" key to move left.
  • Use either the Right Arrow or "D" key to move Right
  • The mouse can also be used for movement. Simply, hover over the arena and play :)

Rules:

The object of the game is to keep the asteroids off of the ground by rebounding them with the bar. You have only enough stamina to drop three asteroids before the game ends. Your Points will be displayed on the screen to the left of the arena.
